FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How often are new coins added?

New coins can be added anytime. Go to the "Add Coins" page and refresh to get the most recent list. Coin requests can be made to: support@coldcrypto.net

Do you store our addresses?

The addresses you input are only stored locally on your device. We do not monitor address, store them anywhere, or attempt to associate them with you. The app requires no account set up so we require no personal information from you.

Feature Requests

If you would like to request a feature please email: support@coldcrypto.net

How can we help?
